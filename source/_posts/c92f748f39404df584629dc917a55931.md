## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 (3-inch/7.5 cm) piece peeled ginger
- [ ] 1 cup (80 g) panko breadcrumbs
- [ ] 2 limes
- [ ] ½ head Napa or Savoy cabbage
- [ ] Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
- [ ] 1 bunch scallions
- [ ] 4 Kishu or 3 Satsuma
- [ ] 2 tablespoons neutral oil, plus more for frying
- [ ] mandarins
- [ ] 1 handful fresh mint leaves
- [ ] ¼ cup (60 ml) unseasoned rice vinegar
- [ ] 2 large eggs, beaten
- [ ] 2 tablespoons toasted
- [ ] sesame oil
- [ ] 1 tablespoon sugar or honey
- [ ] 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- [ ] 1 handful sliced almonds, toasted

## Instructions

1. Slice 2 chicken breasts in half lengthwise into 4 thin pieces. Place on a plastic cutting board, cover them in plastic wrap or parchment paper, and whack them with a rolling pin, wine bottle, or cast-iron skillet to thin them out until they are an even thickness of about ½ inch (12 mm).
2. Make a breading station with two pie plates or shallow bowls. Add 2 eggs to one plate, beating well with a fork or whisk, and 1 cup (80 g) breadcrumbs to the other. Season the breadcrumbs with salt and pepper. Using one hand, and one hand only, dip the chicken first in the egg, and let the excess drip off. Then dip in the breadcrumbs and set aside on a plate. Do this to all of the chicken. Get that cutting board in the sink and wash your hands and clean up anything raw chicken-related.
   In a cast-iron or other large heavy skillet, heat ½ inch (12 mm) oil over medium-high heat. Fry your chicken for 2 to 3 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through. Set aside on a wire cooling rack, sprinkling with a bit more salt.
3. Into a large bowl, grate a 3-inch (7.5 cm) piece peeled ginger with a Microplane to make 2 tablespoons. Add ¼ cup rice vinegar (60 ml), 2 tablespoons sesame oil, 1 tablespoon sugar or honey, juice of 1 lime, and 2 tablespoons
   neutral oil. Season with salt.
4. Core and shred ½ head cabbage using a mandoline. Slice 1 bunch scallions and peel and segment 3 or 4 mandarins. Cut the remaining lime into wedges for serving.
5. Add the cabbage, scallions, mandarins, 1 handful mint leaves, and 1 handful toasted sliced almonds to the bowl with the dressing. Toss well. Put 2 pieces of chicken on each of the 2 serving plates. Top with the salad and serve with the lime wedges alongside.
