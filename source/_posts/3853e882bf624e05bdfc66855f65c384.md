## Ingredients

- [ ] 4 c. flour, heaping
- [ ] 4 eggs (or more) 6?
- [ ] 4 T. sugar, heaping
- [ ] ½ c. water
- [ ] ½ tsp. salt
- [ ] ½ c. milk
- [ ] 1 pkg. yeast
- [ ] ½ cube butter

## Instructions

1. Combine flour, sugar and salt in a bowl. Dissolve yeast in small amount of water (1/2 cup).
2. Beat eggs then add the eggs and liquids (melt butter and add too) in with flour mixture.
3. Mix all ingredients with hands in round motion until soft.
4. Let rise about 1 hour. Use spoon in circular motion to beat down again.
5. Let rise again (½ hour or more) then deep fry.
6. Dough will be sticky, so dip fingers in water and get a small ball of dough and pull apart to make a hole in middle and drop into hot oil or shortening. Brown on both sides; drain on paper towel.
7. While warm, roll in granulated or powdered sugar.
