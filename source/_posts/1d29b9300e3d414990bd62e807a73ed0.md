## Ingredients

- [ ] 5 eggs
- [ ] ¾ c. sugar
- [ ] ½ tsp. salt
- [ ] 1 can evaporated milk
- [ ] 2 c. fresh milk
- [ ] 2 tsp. vanilla
- [ ] ¼ tsp. nutmeg

## Instructions

First scald milk together; beat eggs, sugar, salt and nutmeg for about 3 minutes at high speed, then add the hot milk while beating (so egg mixture will not cook). Add vanilla and mix well. Pour into Pyrex dish and place in pan with hot water. Bake at 350° for about 45 minutes or when knife will come out clean.
