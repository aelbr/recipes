## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1 1/4 tsp baking soda
- [ ] ⅓ cup brown sugar
- [ ] 3 tbs oat bran
- [ ] ¼ cup vegetable oil
- [ ] ½ cup strong coffee
- [ ] 1½ cups bran cereal (such as All-Bran)
- [ ] 1 cup buttermilk
- [ ] ½ cup raisins, dried prunes, or other dried fruit
- [ ] ½ tsp. salt
- [ ] 2¼ cups unsifted white flour

## Instructions

Mix the egg, sugar, oil, coffee, buttermilk, and salt. Sift together the flour and baking soda, stir in the bran and bran cereal, and add this mixture to the liquid ingredients. Stir just until blended. Quickly stir in raisins. Let the batter sit refrigerated overnight. Scoop into prepared muffin tins and bake at 400° for 20 minutes.
